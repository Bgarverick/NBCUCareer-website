Business Services

Our NBCUniversal Business Services organization includes corporate service functions such as environmental health & safety, security & crisis management, global facility operations, transportation, retail operations, energy programs and food services. Servicing clients at NBCUniversals global facilities, the business services team helps coordinate efforts and programs among local operations, while ensuring consistent delivery of a high-level of service to our broad portfolio of internal clients.

East Coast TV & On-Air Operations

Our East Coast TV & On-Air Operations organization supports the production, post-production, and distribution of content across our company's expansive portfolio of news and entertainment brands. Servicing more than broadcast, the East Coast TV & On-Air Operations team provides production, graphics, and distribution support. Helping clients successfully release content across the ever-changing media landscape, it includes TV everywhere, electronic sell through, streaming, and more. The team is comprised of broadcast operations, studio operations, graphics, engineering and facilities.

Field & Production Operations

Our Field & Production Operations is a dynamic and multifaceted organization. It includes News production at 30 Rock as well as all domestic and international news bureaus, satellite transmission and data operations. It incorporates an international team of engineers and camera crews who provide day-to-day technical support and live-remote origination for NBC News worldwide. Striving to be first in class and first on the ground, the expansive editing organization, media management, enhanced streaming techniques and archival departments of NBC News are key to its success. The Field & Production Operations team closely aligns NBC News production with post-production assets. The team also looks to customize production, field and transmission operations across NBCUniversal brands. It provides superior live/ live-to-tape transmission capabilities for NBC News and NBC family members such as CNBC, Telemundo, Access Hollywood, NBC Sports, Weather, NBC Entertainment, and Peacock Productions, as well as for non-NBC customers.

Information Technology

Our Information Technology team is responsible for NBCUniversa's global systems, infrastructure and operations for the company's worldwide media portfolio. Part of the Operations & Technical Services (O&TS) organization, the Information Technology portfolio includes systems for all corporate functions, Domestic and International Film, Home Entertainment, Theme Parks, Cable News & Entertainment, Television Production & Distribution and Global Television Networks. The team strives to provide business technology services and high-tech talent to support the needs and expectations of the various end-users and customer groups enabling them to successfully operate their businesses.

International Operations & Technology

Our International O&TS team supports all business units that form the International division of NBCUniversal. This includes groups that support TV production, channels and distribution, film production and distribution, news, information technology, environmental health and safety and security. The international group works closely with our domestic counterparts, especially in the areas of broadcast operations and IT, on a number of strategic initiatives leveraging the scale of NBCUniversal while capitalising on opportunities in international markets. Media Products & Advanced Technology

Working with all of NBCUniversal's brands, our Media Products & Advanced Technology team provides clients with the tools and technology needed to serve audiences in a digital world. The team strives to ensure better business through better technology by creating tools and talent for our diverse media portfolio, helping brands thrive in our ever-changing technological landscape. Focusing on product strategy, development, design and technical architecture, the client-centric team also evaluates emerging platforms and shares knowledge and best practices across NBCUniversal and business partners. Additionally, the team continues our work in developing industry technology standards; informing regulatory activity and driving research and development in business critical areas.

Studio Operations

Based on the West Coast, our NBCUniversal Studios Operations organization delivers quality operations focusing on talent, technology, and extraordinary customer service. The division offers stages and backlot locations, cutting-edge production and post-production facilities and services, special events, and marketing. Studio Operations is responsible for providing office and production support facilities, as well as maintaining all buildings, grounds, roads, and utility infrastructure. The team supports and partners with both our internal and external clients, while developing and executing a strategic plan that ensures the long-term success of our premier Studio facility. The team works closely with NBCUniversal's theme park division, Universal Studios Hollywood, and our Global Real Estate teams on the development of our strategic Evolution Plan for the Universal Lot. In addition, Studio Operations includes our physical and digital vaults, film preservation, technical services, and Universal Studios Digital Services.With operations oversight of 40 production and post-production support departments and facilities, new technologies for DI, and a virtual stage environment, NBCUniversal Studio Operations is considered the leader in its field.
