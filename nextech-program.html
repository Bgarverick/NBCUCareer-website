Supported by the industry's best-in-class technology and operations center, our hands-on NexTech Program is a two-year technology program designed to train the next generation of media technologists.



Building Technical Skills

Our NexTech program is incredibly powerful and offers the flexibility of working within one primary technical organization of the Operations & Technical Services division while learning multiple aspects of media technology. During the two year experience, participants will rotate throughout the primary organization, building their skills and technical aptitude while learning about various parts of the business.

Top-Notch Television Technology

At the forefront of the television industry’s technology and operations center, we support NBCUniversal’s portfolio of broadcast, cable, and film businesses. Operations and Technology Services includes domestic television production operations, Universal Studio operations, global technical operations, information technology, and media products & advanced technologies. We also perform corporate service functions, including environmental health & safety, facilities, dining services, and security & crisis management.
